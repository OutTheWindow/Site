Greetings

We are ‘Out the Window’, a self-led art collective based in Bristol

We share a studio in Phillips Marsh and are interested in supporting each other in our individual art practices

We have hosted shows in Bristol in Centre space gallery, The Island, Strange Brew, and D-unit.

The Studio cabin in St. Phillips Marsh.

Emily Seffar, Things I wish I bought on Facebook Marketplace, Gouache on paper, 2021

Emily is a miniaturist who will often hunt for the smallest of frames to match her intricately observed collections of furniture, pigeons, and medieval illuminations.

Andrew Wilson, Triptych, Screenprint on paper, 2021

Andrew is a painter and printmaker who combines found collage with striking colours and playful mark-making to create his images, resulting in funny uncanny scenes.

Robert Ive, A field where the horn howls, Plaster etching, 2021

Robert is an artist who makes images of imagined places in various media. His work explores natural and built spaces, often using world-building as a springboard.

Edward Hancox, Deep end #1-#2, Photographic Print, 2022

Edward is a director and photographer whose work often looks at unseen moments of social interaction and drama, pulling back assumed subject matter to show hidden narratives.

Mary Trehearne, still from music video FLEECE x FLEECE, 2021

Mary is a puppeteer and filmmaker who creates wild characters for her absurdist videos, mixing the innocent with the macabre and distorted.

Sarah-Jane Mccarthy, still lives, 2021

Sarah is an artist and animator who runs Figure-drawing lessons and creates animations that bring colour and life to people's diary entries.

Diwas Dewan, Paper Prince, Still of live performance, 2021

Diwas is a performer and filmmaker who is interested in the gesture of performance. Finding the poetic in the small movements of the everyday.
